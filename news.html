    Why did The Weeknd boycott the Grammys?

    More

    After being snubbed by the Recording Academy in 2020, The Weeknd called the organisation 'corrupt'. The singer failed to receive a single nomination despite the mammoth success of his album 'After Hours' and its hit singles, including the record-breaking 'Blinding Lights'. As a result, The Weeknd has boycotted the awards show and will not submit any more music to the Grammys.
